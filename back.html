Back Workouts

Lat Pulldown:

For this exercise there a few instructions to do it right and more importantly not to injure yourself in the process. First, start by adjusting the bench that you are sitting on and make sure that it is adjusted to your height to be able to reach the barbell with a little stretch. Then pick the lightest weight on the machine to pull it down before doing so proceed to having a good shoulder wide grip on the barbell. Secondly, pull down the barbell towrds your chin and make sure to not go any lower than that. The point of this exerciseis to work your lat muscles which are located on your back under your armpits. So make sure you try to squeeze well enough under while pulling down and bringing the barbell back up slowly but not placing the weight back. Try the exercise for a set of 8 reps without weights first and then try it with weights gradually. Click here for more info!

Rows:

For this exercise there a few instructions to do it right and more importantly not to injure yourself in the process. The point of this exercise is to work you lats and lower back more but your entire back would be at work for this exercise including your biceps. Sit on the bench with your arms stretched a bit to grab the handle and then pull the handles towards yourself into your abdomen area but not quite touching it. Try it without any weights to be able to get the form correctly and then slowly bring the handle away from yourself until right before you place down the weight to still have resistance in play. Once you get comfortable after the first set add weights gradually to be able to lift more and activate your muscles more. Click here for more info!
