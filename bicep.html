Bicep Workouts

Hammer curls:

For this exercise there a few instructions to do it right and more importantly not to injure yourself in the process. Firstly, pick then easiest dumbell that you can carry and in a standing position hold the dumbells in a hammer position as shown on the image on the right. Then lift the dumbells from your side to a 90 degree angle on your arms, then proceed to lower the dumbells back to the original position slowly to get a good squeeze in. Finally, finish your first set and move up by 5 pounds or more depends on you to do another set for a set of 3. Click here for a quick video!

Bicep Curls:

For this exercise there a few instructions to do it right and more importantly not to injure yourself in the process. First pick then easiest dumbell that you can carry and in a standing position hold the dumbells in a 180 position as shown on the image on the left hand side. Make sure lift the dumbells from your side to a 90 degree angle on your arms, then proceed to lower the dumbells back to the original position slowly to get a good squeeze in.
